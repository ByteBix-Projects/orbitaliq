Our Training Philosophy

We firmly believe that learning GIS and Remote Sensing requires working directly on real telemetry. That is why our training modules bypass canned datasets. Instead, students extract and analyze live Sentinel-1, Sentinel-2, Landsat-8, Landsat-9, MODIS, and ERA5 spatial imagery grids for their target research areas, guided closely by PhD scholars and spatial developers.
