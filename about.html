Corporate Profile

A Research-First Geospatial Consultancy

OrbitalIQ Analytics Pvt. Ltd. was founded to bridge the gap between complex Earth Observation telemetry and actionable decision-making. We believe that satellite imagery, aerial data, and spatial maps hold the key to solving the most pressing challenges in environment, climate, water, and food security.

By combining rigorous scientific research methodologies with scalable, high-performance GeoAI systems, we deliver maps and analytics of exceptional fidelity. We serve government entities looking for urban planning metrics, NGOs executing resource watershed management, and researchers searching for verified analytical pipelines.

Our Mission

To democratize Earth Observation datasets by transforming complex, high-volume telemetry into clear, scientific, and actionable spatial intelligence.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in GeoAI research and climate analytics, empowering sustainable, data-driven stewardship of natural resources.

Why We Excel

What Makes OrbitalIQ Different?

We operate at the intersection of scientific research, enterprise technology, and practical training.

Research-Driven Approach

We don't just process images; we validate spatial datasets against academic peer-reviewed parameters and physical ground truth to guarantee absolute reliability.

Technology-First Operations

Our stack is built on modern high-performance tools: Google Earth Engine, Python (Rasterio, GDAL), PyTorch, PostGIS, and modern cloud database architecture.

End-to-End Capabilities

From acquiring raw multispectral telemetry and processing complex imagery pipelines to hosting customized web-GIS consoles for our B2B partners.
