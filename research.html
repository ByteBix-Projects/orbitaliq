Scientific Outreach

Academic & Institutional Collaboration

At OrbitalIQ, research is not a secondary activity—it is the foundation of our company. We collaborate with universities, research labs, state institutes, and international NGOs to study global ecological, hydrologic, and climatic cycles.

Our scientific team actively contributes to peer-reviewed international journals, presents methodologies at global GIS conferences, and authors technical feasibility reports that direct conservation funds.

Collaborative Domains

• Global Climate Modeling (ERA5 reanalysis integrations)

• Sub-basin Watershed Delineations & Erosion Audits

• Multi-temporal Crop Classification algorithms (GeoAI)

• Web GIS portals deployment for academic organizations
