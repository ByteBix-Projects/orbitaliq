A Culture of Discovery & Geospatial Innovation

At OrbitalIQ, we work at the forefront of space technology, hydrology, agriculture, and deep learning. Our team consists of motivated software engineers, PhD researchers, and spatial analysts who enjoy addressing challenging global problems.

We foster a research-first environment that emphasizes continuous learning, publication support, and tools flexibility. If you are passionate about satellite telemetry, spatial scripting, and environmental conservation, we would love to have you on board.

Why Join OrbitalIQ?

• Impactful Work: Build systems that audit water resources and track global climate shifts.

• High-Performance Tools: Work on cloud GPU infrastructure, GEE, and advanced raster databases.

• Career Progression: Direct mentorship from PhD scholars, paper authoring support, and conference sponsorship.
